About MDOAT

Milwaukee Density Opportunity Analysis Tool (MDOAT) is a web-based GIS application identifying parcels in the City of Milwaukee where current zoning constrains higher-density development. Built for municipal planning staff, housing policy analysts, and urban development advocates.

Data Sources

  • Parcels + MPROP — City of Milwaukee Open Data Portal (nightly)
  • Zoning Districts — City of Milwaukee (nightly)
  • Vacant Lots — City of Milwaukee (nightly)
  • Neighborhoods — City of Milwaukee (nightly)
  • Opportunity Zones — City of Milwaukee (nightly)
  • Tax Incremental Districts — City of Milwaukee (nightly)
  • Redevelopment Plans — City of Milwaukee (nightly)
  • User Annotations — AGOL hosted feature layer (real-time)
